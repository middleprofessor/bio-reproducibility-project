Mean body length (in mm), developmental stage and trophic group of arthropod morpho-species collected on the vegetation at Jonkershoek 
on December 2014 (six year old vegetation (post-fire age)) for a "bird exclosure" experiment. 

Trophic groups: "Herb" = herbivore; "Pred" = intermediate predator
